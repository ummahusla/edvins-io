Senior Front-End Engineer with 12+ years of experience building product-focused web applications with React and TypeScript. I specialise in frontend architecture, design systems, developer experience, and shipping complex interfaces that remain maintainable as products and teams scale. Across startups and established teams, I have helped turn early product ideas into robust platforms by defining frontend foundations, introducing engineering standards, and working closely with product and design.

After hours, I work on various [side projects](/projects), explore new technologies, and [write about code](/blog). One of my recent projects is [Albion Online Grind](https://albiononlinegrind.com), a tool that helps players improve their gameplay in the popular sandbox MMORPG, Albion Online. I also founded [Stealth Labs](https://www.stealthlabs.dev/), an independent software consulting entity for remote product and engineering work.

I'm a passionate advocate of the ["learning in public"](https://www.swyx.io/learn-in-public/) movement and track my [learning journey](/self-education). I'm always looking for ways to improve my skills and knowledge, and I'm not afraid to share my experiences (even the failures) with the world.
